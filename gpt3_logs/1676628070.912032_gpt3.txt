RESPONSE:

Stripe Elements is a set of tools that allows businesses to accept payments directly from their customers. It includes a payment gateway, a payment processor, and a merchant account. businesses can use Stripe Connect to facilitate direct payments between other businesses and their own customers. Stripe Connect allows businesses to onboard, verify, and pay out to sellers or service providers. It also enables businesses to add money to their account and pay out their sellers or service providers.
