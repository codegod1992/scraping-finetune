DETAILED ANSWER:

==========

RESPONSE:

Stripe is a platform that enables businesses to accept payments from customers and pay out to sellers or service providers. Connect is a tool that allows businesses to route payments between multiple parties. The Connect API enables businesses to create their own custom onboarding experience or flow. The Stripe Shell is a browser-based shell with the Stripe CLI pre-installed. Login to Stripedocs and press Control + Backtick on your keyboard to start managing your Stripe resources in test mode.
