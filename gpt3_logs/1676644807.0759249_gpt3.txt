Stripe is a software platform that allows businesses to accept online payments. Stripe offers a variety of features, including online invoicing, subscription management, and payment processing. Stripe also offers a "Payment Link" feature that allows businesses to share payment links with customers via social media, email, or their website. Payment Links supports over 30 languages and over 20 payment methods.
