Stripe is a developer platform that allows businesses to accept and manage online payments. Stripe provides a set of tools and services that businesses can use to integrate online payments into their websites and applications. Stripe also offers a number of features that businesses can use to manage their payments, such as invoicing, recurring payments, and fraud prevention.
