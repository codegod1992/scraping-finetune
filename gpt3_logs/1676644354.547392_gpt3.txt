Stripe is a software platform that allows businesses to accept and process payments online. Stripe offers a variety of payment processing options, including credit and debit card payments, bank transfers, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Stripe also offers a subscription service that allows businesses to set up recurring billing for their customers.
